    Mexico

    Is a country of wonders. We have a huge natural wealth and a huge cultural wealth. Of more than 190 countries in the world, Mexico is among the top five in terms of its numbers of plants and animals and ecosystems.


    We have organisms from those that can only be seen with a microscope, to the enormous blue whale, the largest animal to have existed on the planet.

    The more native species of flora and fauna we have, the more options to enjoy, investigate, cure, eat, drink, and build, we have. This is why, the most important asset of Mexico is its flora and fauna.

    Why is there so much wealth?

    There are six reasons why Mexico has so much natural wealth.

    Mexico is in

    1

     

    Mexico is in the tropics where the climate is less extreme.

    Mexico is full of

    2

    Mexico is full of mountains that produce different soils, climates and environments.

    Mexico is surrounded by

    3

    Mexico is surrounded by seas of different temperatures and depths.

    Flora and fauna

    4

    In Mexico, the flora and fauna of North America (like the raccoon) meet those of South America (like the opossum).

    Mexico is big enough

    5

    Mexico is big enough to be the 14th biggest country in the world.

    Mexico is home to

    6

    Mexico is home to many indigenous cultures that, over thousands of years, have produced many varieties of maize, beans, pumpkin, chilli and many other species we eat.
